Centamark is a high-end multi-purpose development which positions itself as the ‘Pulse of Tianjin’. Keeping this in mind, rhythmic lines are used to create the logo mark ‘M’, evoking a sense of motion and pulse. Also, the huge M provides gravity and helps anchor the brand as the landmark of the city.

This design was one of the options for the client.

OutLoud Too is an anthology of English poems by Hong Kong poets. Though the poems’ themes are different, all of the poems are united by sentiments towards Hong Kong culture. Such a sensibility is highlighted in the design of the book, by using a neon sign as visual representation - a uniquely local feature of the streets of Hong Kong.

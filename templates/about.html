ABOUT

My name is Chip, I am one of those fortunate souls who managed to make my interest into my career. I graduated as a valedictorian from School of Design, The HongKong Polytechnic University in 2012. For the next two years, I worked as a designer in the creative agency Alchemy Asia, serving numerous clients such as The Economist, HKUST, and Kowloon Dairy. I like to explore the possibilities of different mediums and I am a firm believer that design is wit made visible.

On occasions when I am not at my desk designing, you may find me in a cinema, a comic convention, or somewhere in the world eating.
